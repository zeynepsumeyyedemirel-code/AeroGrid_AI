# Renewable Energy Field Safety and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Rules

## Working at Heights Procedure
- Before climbing the wind turbine tower, the Vertical Fall Arrest System and Dual-Leg Lanyard safety harness must be inspected.
- Climbing the turbine tower OR working outdoors is STRICTLY PROHIBITED when wind speeds exceed 12 m/s.

## Emergency Communication
- On-site emergency radio channel: Channel 4.
- First aid kits are located in the main control building and service vehicles.
